YouTube Channel - Wristwatch Revival

Often called, the "Bob Ross" of watch repair, Marshall Sutcliffe's popular YouTube channel focuses solely on repairing watches. A common theme throughout the comments of his videos are viewers who state that while they "aren't into watches," they very much enjoy his relaxing style and narration, and find his videos both fascinating and stress relieving.


Restoration of a $100,000 Rolex GMT From 1958!

"Restoration of a $100,000 Rolex GMT From 1958!" YouTube, uploaded by Wristwatch Revival, 23 Nov 2023, https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zQhHQ7pkBf8"

Marshall repairs a very valuable Rolex GMT Master 6542 from 1958, that is in extremly poor condition. This model watch normally goes for around $100,000.


This Beat Up, Non-Running Omega Seamaster Has Big Potential! Vintage Watch Restoration

"This Beat Up, Non-Running Omega Seamaster Has Big Potential! Vintage Watch Restoration." YouTube, uploaded by Wristwatch Revival, 28 Feb 2022, https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KY8nU7BOM34

Marshall took a chance on a Omega Seamaster 120 from eBay. The watchface is barely visible through the scratched crystal, and the watch itself does not run. Watch (pun intended) as he brings this expensive timepiece back to life!


Seattle YouTube star restores watches for millions of viewers!

"Seattle YouTube star restores watches for millions of viewers!" YouTube, uploaded by King5Evening, 15 Apr 2022, https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_NCEmCxLIt0

King 5's Evening Magazine did a profile of Marshall Sutcliffe, a popular YouTube content creator from Seattle, WA. Fans of his YouTube page often call him the "Bob Ross of watch repair."
